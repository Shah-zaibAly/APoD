About APoD

APoD brings you NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day — a curated daily image or video of our universe, each accompanied by a brief explanation written by a professional astronomer.

Since June 16, 1995, APOD has been one of the most popular websites on the internet, inspiring millions to look up and wonder.
